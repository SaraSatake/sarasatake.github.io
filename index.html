About Me

Hello! I am currently a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Tsukuba, Japan.

I am a member of the Laboratory of Evolutionary Computation, and my research interests include stigmergy, emergent communication, collective behavior, and cognition.

To explore these topics, I use computational multi-agent simulations to study the emergence of complex behaviors and interactions.
